## COMMON OBJECTIONS ACROSS ALL PERSONAS

### Pricing Transparency
> "What's your pricing model exactly?" / "Why don't you disclose your fee?"

**Response:** "We bake our fee into the budget so you get true raw cost of the lead. Your leads will be $250-350 in California, about $200-250 in other states. The case cost is what matters - and that's consistently $1,700-3,500 depending on state and criteria."

### Media Buy vs Pay-Per-Lead
> "I prefer pay per lead because I can pinpoint issues"

**Response:** "I understand. We used to offer pay-per-lead and still do for some clients at $350/lead. The tradeoff is you'll pay about 30% more per case. Most clients prefer our campaign model once they see the first batch results. Happy to start with PPL if that builds trust."

### Past Vendor Failures
> "I've heard this pitch before and been burned"

**Response:** "I hear that a lot. Can I ask who burned you? [Listen]. Yeah, [Quintessa/Walker/whoever] has that reputation. Here's what's different - we don't do calculators, fake testimonials, or round-robin leads. Everything is exclusive, and our intake partner Patrick is a former director of intake - he knows what a real case looks like."

### Why Should I Trust You?
> "Why should I trust you over anyone else?"

**Response:** "Fair question. We've worked with Angel Reyes for 3-4 years, Jacobi, Wilshire... I can connect you with Patrick at Inquire to Esquire who you can talk criteria with. But honestly, the proof is in the first batch. We don't do long-term contracts - if it doesn't work, you don't continue."
