## KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

1. **Media buy > pay per lead** - Better margins, fresher leads
2. **Dedicated intake partner is critical** - Patrick model works
3. **Pixel seasoning matters** - Established accounts convert better
4. **1-year freshness filter** - Dramatically improves conversion
5. **Exclusive leads mandatory** - Shared leads are worthless
6. **SB-37 compliance** - California requires proper disclosures
7. **Hispanic market opportunity** - Walker/Los Defensores dominate
