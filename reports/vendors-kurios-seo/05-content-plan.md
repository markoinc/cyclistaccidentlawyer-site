## PAGE TEMPLATE STRUCTURES

### Vendor Review Template
```
1. Overview & Quick Facts (100 words)
2. Company Background (200 words)
3. Services Offered (300 words)
4. Pricing Model (300 words)
5. Pros and Cons (bullet list)
6. Customer Reviews (aggregated)
7. Our Verdict (200 words)
8. Alternatives (links to other reviews)
9. FAQ (5-7 questions)
10. CTA: "Get Matched with the Right Vendor"
```

### State Guide Template
```
1. State Market Overview (200 words)
2. Key Regulations (300 words)
3. Average Lead Costs in [State] (table)
4. Top Vendors Operating in [State] (500 words)
5. State-Specific Challenges (300 words)
6. Success Tips for [State] (bullet list)
7. FAQ (5 questions)
8. CTA: "Find [State] MVA Lead Vendors"
```
