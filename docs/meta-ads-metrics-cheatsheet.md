## B2B Lead Gen Specific Notes

1. **Quality > Quantity:** A $100 lead that closes at 20% beats a $30 lead that closes at 2%.

2. **Track downstream:** Connect Meta to your CRM. Know which campaigns produce *revenue*, not just leads.

3. **Longer attribution:** B2B sales cycles are 30-90+ days. Use longer attribution windows (7-day click, 1-day view minimum).

4. **Retargeting is gold:** ThruPlay and page visitor audiences convert 3-5x better than cold.

5. **Lead form vs. landing page:** Instant Forms get more leads but lower quality. Landing pages get fewer but better leads.
