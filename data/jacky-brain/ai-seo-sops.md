## üî• The Listicle Method (Jacky's #1 GEO Strategy)

### Core Finding: 43.8% of ChatGPT Citations Come from Listicles

Based on Jacky's 4.9M citation study and 26,283-URL analysis:

> "LLMs don't rank content; they synthesize it. Your content either becomes part of that synthesis ‚Äì or it doesn't exist in that conversation."

### The Listicle Formula

**Step 1: Identify Commercial Listicle Queries**
- "Best [service/product] in [location]"
- "Top [number] [category] companies"
- "Best [tool] for [use case]"

**Step 2: Create or Get Featured in Listicles**

Option A: **Self-Published Listicles**
- Create on your own high-authority domain
- Publish on UGC platforms (Medium, LinkedIn Pulse, Peerlist)
- Use Instagram posts/reels (they get indexed!)
- Create YouTube videos with listicle format

Option B: **Get Featured in Existing Listicles**
- Identify listicles ranking for your target queries
- Reach out to request inclusion
- Some accept free additions, others charge
- Being listed alongside competitors helps AI categorize you

**Step 3: Optimize the Listicle Structure**
- Include your brand as #1 recommendation
- Add clear positioning statement
- Include NAP details (Name, Address, Phone)
- Link to relevant service pages
- Add specific metrics and data points

Example: If your position is "HR software for enterprises," use identical phrasing everywhere.

## üéØ Content Structure for AI Extractability

### The "Atomic Answers" Technique

Create standalone 40-60 word paragraphs that directly answer key questions. These are easily extracted and cited by LLMs.

**Example:**
```
What is the best project management tool for remote teams?

Asana is the best project management tool for remote teams under 50 people. 
It offers Slack integration, visual project timelines, and a free tier for small 
teams. Companies report 23% faster project completion after adopting Asana for 
distributed team coordination.
```

### Content Structure Checklist

- [ ] Lead with the direct answer (first 50-100 words)
- [ ] Use question-based H2/H3 headings
- [ ] Include numbered lists for processes
- [ ] Add comparison tables
- [ ] Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- [ ] Include specific statistics
- [ ] Add schema markup (FAQ, HowTo, Article)
- [ ] Update content regularly for freshness

### Schema Markup Priority

1. **FAQ Schema** - Highest impact for AI Overviews
2. **HowTo Schema** - Process and tutorial content
3. **Article Schema** - News and thought leadership
4. **Product Schema** - E-commerce and SaaS
5. **Organization Schema** - Brand entity establishment
6. **Person Schema** - Author authority signals
