## ü§ñ GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Strategy for 2026

### What is GEO?
**Generative Engine Optimization** = optimizing content to appear in AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Claude).

### GEO vs Traditional SEO:
| Traditional SEO | GEO |
|-----------------|-----|
| Rank pages | Get cited in AI answers |
| Clicks to website | Brand mentioned without click |
| Keyword targeting | Entity/concept optimization |
| Backlink quantity | Authority density |
| Traffic metrics | Citation tracking |

### Top GEO Tactics for Local Businesses:

#### 1. Create "Best Of" Listicles
- **43.8% of ChatGPT citations** come from listicle content
- Create your own: "Best [Service] in [City] 2026"
- Get featured in others' listicles (local news, bloggers)
- **Unstructured citations** (mentions in "Best of [City]" even WITHOUT links) now stronger than directory profiles

#### 2. Answer Engine Optimization
- Write conversational FAQ content
- Structure content for AI summarization
- Use clear headers, bullet points, tables
- Target question-based queries

#### 3. Build Topical Authority
- Create content clusters around your services
- Connect concepts (not just keywords)
- AI seeks **connected concepts**, not keyword density
- Studies show **no correlation** between keyword density and AI rankings

#### 4. Entity Optimization
- Consistent NAP across ALL platforms
- Schema markup (LocalBusiness, Service, Review, FAQ, Event)
- Wikipedia/Wikidata presence (if possible)
- Directory listings for LLM recognition

#### 5. Content Depth Matters Most
> "When it comes to securing AI mentions and citations, content depth (sentence and word counts) and readability matter most, while traditional SEO metrics like traffic and backlinks have little impact." ‚Äî Growth Memo, March 2025

## ‚≠ê Review Generation Strategies

### Tier List (Best to Worst):
1. **In-person ask** - Highest conversion rate
2. **QR codes at checkout** - Easy, passive
3. **Follow-up email/SMS** - Automated, scalable
4. **Review link in signature** - Low effort, consistent
5. **Social media ask** - Lower conversion

### Review Generation Best Practices:
- Ask at peak satisfaction moment (right after service)
- Make it EASY - direct link to review page
- Don't incentivize (violates TOS)
- Diversify platforms (Google, Yelp, Facebook, industry-specific)
- Respond to ALL reviews within 24-48 hours

### Handling Negative Reviews:
1. Respond calmly and professionally
2. Apologize if appropriate
3. Offer to make it right
4. Take conversation offline
5. Never be defensive or argumentative

### Review Stats to Know:
- 89% of consumers would use a business that responds to all reviews
- 62% avoid businesses with incorrect info online
- 4.9 rating performs better than 5.0 (more believable)
- Only Google and reviewer can delete reviews
