# Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization

## SOP 1: New GBP Setup (EP 339, EP 173)
**Local SEO SOP for a new GMB**

### Steps:
1. **Claim/Create GBP**
   - Use real business address or legit SAB (Service Area Business)
   - Match business name EXACTLY to legal name (no keyword stuffing initially)

2. **Complete All Fields**
   - Primary category: Choose most specific relevant category
   - Secondary categories: Add 2-4 related categories
   - Business description: 750 characters, include primary keywords naturally
   - Services: Add all services with descriptions
   - Products: Add if applicable

3. **Add Photos**
   - Logo (square format)
   - Cover photo
   - Interior/exterior photos
   - Team photos
   - Product/service photos
   - GEO-tag all photos with business location

4. **Set Hours**
   - Be accurate - wrong hours = bad reviews
   - Include special hours for holidays

5. **Add Attributes**
   - All applicable attributes (Black-owned, Women-owned, etc.)
   - Payment methods
   - Accessibility features

## SOP 2: Buying/Acquiring GBP Locations (EP 531)
**Is buying a Google location (GBP) worth it?**

### Decision Framework:
| Factor | Buy GBP | Build New |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Existing reviews | ✅ If positive | ❌ If negative |
| Age of listing | ✅ 1+ year old | ❌ Brand new |
| Price | Under $500 | Over $1000 |
| Category match | Exact match | Different niche |

### Steps to Acquire:
1. Find sellers on Swapd, BHW, or Facebook groups
2. Verify reviews are real (not fake patterns)
3. Check for existing penalties (look at map pack history)
4. Complete ownership transfer properly
5. Gradually update info (don't change everything at once)

## SOP 3: Ranking SABs - Service Area Businesses (EP 530)
**Ranking a business with no location**

### Steps:
1. **Setup as SAB in GBP**
   - Hide address, show service areas only
   - Define realistic service radius

2. **Create Location Signals**
   - City-specific landing pages on website
   - Local phone numbers (Google Voice for each area)
   - Citations mentioning service areas

3. **Build Hyperlocal Content**
   - "[Service] in [Neighborhood]" pages
   - Local case studies
   - Community involvement posts

4. **Geo-Targeted Links**
   - Local directory citations
   - Local news/press mentions
   - Chamber of commerce listings

# Map Pack / Snack Pack Ranking

## SOP 4: Snack Pack in 4 Days (EP 436)
**Fast Map Pack Ranking Strategy**

### Prerequisites:
- Verified GBP
- Complete profile (100%)
- Website with local optimization

### Steps:
1. **Day 1: Foundation**
   - Verify all NAP is consistent
   - Add 10 high-authority citations
   - Request reviews from 3-5 existing customers

2. **Day 2: On-Page**
   - Local schema on all pages
   - City + keyword in title tags
   - Embed Google Map on contact page
   - Add directions link

3. **Day 3: Links & Citations**
   - Submit to 20 more citations
   - Get 2-3 local backlinks (sponsors, chambers)
   - Post on GBP with keywords

4. **Day 4: Engagement Signals**
   - Run CTR software (if using) - see CTR section
   - Request more reviews
   - GBP post with offer

## SOP 5: Ranked #1 on Google Maps in 5 Days (EP 497)
**Accelerated Map Pack Strategy**

### Steps:
1. **Optimize GBP Photos**
   - 20+ photos minimum
   - All geo-tagged
   - Include keyword in file names

2. **Review Velocity**
   - Get 3-5 reviews per day for first week
   - Respond to ALL reviews within 24 hours

3. **Google Posts**
   - Daily posts for first 2 weeks
   - Include keywords and CTAs
   - Add images to every post

4. **Direction Boost (EP 677)**
   - Drive direction searches to business
   - Creates engagement signals
   - Use phone farm or CTR service

## SOP 6: Direction Boost Method (EP 677)
**"We can finally GUARANTEE Google Maps rankings"**

### How It Works:
- Simulates users searching for directions to your business
- Creates "driving directions" engagement signals
- Google sees demand = higher rankings

### Implementation:
1. Use CTR software with direction feature
2. Set searches from multiple locations within service area
3. 5-10 direction searches per day
4. Vary timing and source locations

# Local Citations & NAP

## SOP 7: NAP Consistency Test (EP 386)
**"I tested if NAP = Local SEO rankings"**

### Finding: NAP consistency DOES impact rankings

### Audit Process:
1. **Check Top 50 Citations**
   - Use Whitespark or BrightLocal
   - Export all NAP variations found

2. **Identify Inconsistencies**
   - Different phone formats
   - Suite/unit number variations
   - Old addresses
   - Business name variations

3. **Fix Priority Order**
   - Google Business Profile (first)
   - Apple Maps
   - Yelp
   - Facebook
   - Data aggregators (Factual, Neustar, etc.)
   - Industry-specific directories

4. **Update Formula**
   - Exact match on business name
   - Same phone format everywhere
   - Full address including suite
   - Same categories across platforms

## SOP 8: Citations Without an Address (EP 440)
**For SABs or Privacy-Conscious Businesses**

### Steps:
1. Use city/region only (not full address)
2. Keep phone consistent
3. List service areas instead of address
4. Use PO Box ONLY if GBP allows

# Local Keyword Research

## SOP 17: Local Keyword Strategy
**Compiled from multiple episodes**

### Primary Keyword Formula:
`[Service] + [City]` = Target keyword

Examples:
- "plumber austin"
- "personal injury lawyer houston"
- "dentist near me"

### Long-Tail Variations:
- "best [service] in [city]"
- "[service] [neighborhood]"
- "[service] near [landmark]"
- "emergency [service] [city]"
- "[service] open now [city]"

### Keyword Research Process:
1. **Seed Keywords**
   - Main services
   - Service areas

2. **Expand with Tools**
   - Google Keyword Planner
   - Ahrefs/SEMrush local filter
   - Google autocomplete
   - "People also ask"

3. **Competitor Analysis**
   - What are map pack winners targeting?
   - What keywords do their GMB posts use?

4. **Local Modifiers**
   - Neighborhoods
   - Zip codes
   - Nearby landmarks
   - "Near me" variants

## SOP 18: Ranking for "Near Me" Keywords (EP 273)
**Most Asked Local SEO Question**

### Findings:
- GBP proximity is #1 factor
- Website "near me" optimization has limited impact
- Mobile optimization critical

### Strategy:
1. Optimize GBP (not website) for near me
2. Ensure mobile-first website
3. Multiple GBP locations if possible
4. Schema markup with geo-coordinates

## SOP 21: Cold Calling 731 Businesses (EP 581)
**What a Subscriber Learned**

### Script Framework:
"Hi, is this [Owner Name]? This is [Your Name] with [Company]. I specialize in helping [niche] businesses in [city] get more customers from Google. I noticed you're not showing up when people search for [service] - is that something you'd be interested in fixing?"

### Objection Handlers:
- "We already have someone" → "Great! When's your contract up? I'd love to show you what you're missing."
- "Not interested" → "No worries. Quick question - are you getting at least 10 calls a week from Google?"
- "How much?" → "Depends on your competition, but typically $500-2000/month. Want me to send you a free audit first?"

# CTR Manipulation for Local

## SOP 26: CTR Software Setup (EP 213)
**DON'T GET CLAPPED**

### Safe Setup:
1. **Use Residential Proxies**
   - Never datacenter
   - Rotate IPs constantly
   - Geo-target to service area

2. **Search Patterns**
   - Mix branded and non-branded
   - Include maps searches
   - Vary time of day
   - Mobile and desktop mix

3. **Engagement Signals**
   - Scroll on site
   - Click multiple pages
   - Stay 2-5 minutes
   - Some bounces (realistic)

### Volume:
- Start with 10-20 searches/day
- Increase slowly over weeks
- Don't spike during updates

## SOP 27: Local SEO CTR - Does it Work? (EP 427)
**"Local SEO CTR manipulation doesn't work?!"**

### Findings:
- Works BETTER for local than regular SEO
- Direction searches > regular searches
- Combine with phone calls/driving directions
- GBP engagement signals matter most

### Best CTR Actions:
1. Search → Click GBP → Get Directions
2. Search → Click GBP → Call
3. Search → Click GBP → Website → Browse
4. Search → Click GBP → Reviews

## SOP 28: Black Hat CTR Hack (EP 380)
**THE ULTIMATE Method**

### Phone Farm Method:
1. Buy 10-20 cheap Android phones
2. Use different SIM cards
3. Search for business daily
4. Click, get directions, call
5. Rotate locations (drive around)

### Automation Alternative:
1. Use CTR service with phone simulation
2. Request directions feature
3. Simulate calls to tracking number
