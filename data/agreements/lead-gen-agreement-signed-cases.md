### 7. ATTRITION & REPLACEMENT POLICY

**a) Early Attrition Credit**

If a signed case terminates representation within **fourteen (14) days** of signing due to:
- Lead was already represented (misrepresentation)
- No actual injury existed
- Incident was fabricated
- Lead was not the actual claimant

Provider will provide a **replacement case** at no additional cost.

**b) NOT Eligible for Replacement**

- Client declines case after review (case met criteria)
- Claimant fires Client after consultation
- Case dismissed for legitimate legal reasons
- Claimant relocates or becomes unreachable
- Normal attrition after 14-day window

**c) Replacement Process**

- Client notifies Provider within 14 days of discovery
- Provides documentation of disqualifying issue
- Provider reviews and approves/denies within 5 business days
- If approved, replacement case delivered from pipeline

**d) Replacement Cap**

Total replacements shall not exceed **fifteen percent (15%)** of delivered cases per campaign.

**There is NO automatic renewal.** Client must affirmatively order additional campaigns.

**c) Pause & Termination**

- Either party may pause with 7 days written notice
- Pausing does not void guarantee—cases in pipeline will be delivered
- Termination for material breach requires 10 business days cure period

**d) Refund on Termination**

If Client terminates before completion:
- Cases in pipeline will be signed and delivered (signing fee applies)
- Unused media budget refunded minus 20% administrative fee
