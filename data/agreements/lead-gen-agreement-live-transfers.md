### 6. REFUND & REPLACEMENT POLICY

**a) Transfers Eligible for Credit:**

Provider will credit the $100 transfer fee for transfers that:
- Disconnect before Client answers
- Are clearly not the qualified lead (bait and switch)
- Do not meet agreed qualification criteria upon Client's review
- Are duplicate transfers (same person within 30 days)

**b) Underlying Leads:**

The base lead generation cost follows the same replacement policy as raw leads:
- Wrong/disconnected numbers
- Bot/spam submissions
- Already represented
- Outside geography
- Property damage only

**c) NOT Eligible for Credit:**

- Transfer who speaks with Client but doesn't retain
- Transfer who retains competitor after consultation
- Transfer becomes unresponsive after initial transfer
- Case doesn't meet Client's internal criteria beyond agreed qualifications

**d) Process:**
- Client must submit credit requests within **five (5) business days**
- Include transfer date/time, lead name, reason for dispute
- Provider will review call recording and respond within 3 business days

**e) Credit Cap:**
Total credits shall not exceed **twenty-five percent (25%)** of monthly transfer fees.
