## The Speed to Lead SMS Playbook

### Step 1: Automate the First Touch

**The Problem:** Humans cannot physically react in seconds to every lead.

**The Solution:** Automated instant SMS triggered by lead submission.

#### Implementation:

1. **Connect your lead sources to your SMS platform:**
   - Website forms → CRM → SMS automation
   - Facebook/Google Lead Ads → Zapier/Make → SMS
   - Landing pages → Direct integration → SMS

2. **Create instant auto-response workflow:**
   ```
   TRIGGER: New lead created
   DELAY: 0 seconds (immediate)
   ACTION: Send personalized SMS
   ```

3. **Example Instant Response:**
   ```
   Hey {{first_name}}! This is {{rep_name}} from [Business]. 
   I just saw your request come through. 
   I'm available right now - want me to give you a quick call?
   ```

### Step 2: Eliminate Cherry-Picking with Intelligent Routing

**The Problem:** Reps naturally gravitate toward the "best-looking" leads, letting others go cold.

**The Solution:** Dynamic, prioritized lead queues.

#### Implementation:

1. **Score leads automatically:**
   - Lead source (demo request = hot, whitepaper = warm)
   - Company size (enterprise = high priority)
   - Engagement signals (multiple page views)

2. **Route by priority, not preference:**
   - Hot leads → Immediate human callback + SMS
   - Warm leads → Automated SMS sequence → rep follow-up
   - Cold leads → Nurture sequence

3. **Round-robin distribution:**
   - Leads distributed evenly across available reps
   - No manual selection = no cherry-picking

### Step 3: Multi-Channel Speed Attack

Don't rely on just one channel. Hit leads on multiple fronts simultaneously:

#### Immediate (0-60 seconds):
```
1. Auto-SMS: "Hi {{name}}, got your request! Calling you now..."
2. Auto-dial: Power dialer calls lead immediately
3. Auto-email: Confirmation with calendar link
```

#### If No Answer (5 minutes):
```
4. SMS: "Tried calling but missed you. When's a better time?"
5. Voicemail drop: Pre-recorded personalized message
```

#### Continued Follow-up (15-60 minutes):
```
6. SMS with value: "While you're thinking about it, here's a quick case study..."
7. Email with social proof: Client testimonials
```

### Step 4: After-Hours Speed to Lead

Leads don't stop at 5pm. Here's how to maintain speed 24/7:

#### Automated After-Hours Response:
```
Hi {{first_name}}! Thanks for reaching out to [Business]. 

I'm {{rep_name}} and I'll be helping you. I know it's outside 
business hours, but I wanted you to know I got your message.

I'll call you first thing tomorrow morning at 9am - does that work? 
Or reply with a better time.
```

#### Why This Works:
- Immediate acknowledgment (they know you're responsive)
- Sets clear expectation (they'll wait for your call)
- Gives them control (can suggest alternative time)
- Dramatically improves next-day answer rates

## Speed to Lead SMS Templates

### Instant Response Templates

**Hot Lead (Demo Request):**
```
{{first_name}}, awesome! I just got your demo request. 
I'm {{rep_name}} and I specialize in helping companies like yours. 
Free to chat for 2 min right now?
```

**Warm Lead (Content Download):**
```
Hey {{first_name}}! Saw you downloaded our [Resource Name]. 
Hope it's helpful! Any questions I can answer while it's fresh?
```

**Referral Lead:**
```
Hi {{first_name}}! {{referrer_name}} mentioned you might benefit 
from what we do. They spoke highly of you! Got a minute to chat?
```

### Follow-Up Templates (If No Response)

**5 Minutes - Gentle Nudge:**
```
{{first_name}}, I tried calling but think you might be busy. 
Totally get it! What's a better time?
```

**15 Minutes - Add Value:**
```
Quick thought while I have you: Most of our clients in [industry] 
see [specific result]. Worth a 5-min chat to see if that applies to you?
```

**1 Hour - Create Urgency:**
```
{{first_name}}, I have a few minutes open right now if you're free. 
Otherwise I'll try again tomorrow morning. Which works better?
```

**24 Hours - Last Attempt:**
```
Hey {{first_name}}, didn't want to keep bugging you! 

If now isn't the right time, just say so - no pressure. 

But if you're still interested, reply "YES" and I'll call you when 
it's convenient.
```

## Measuring Speed to Lead

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Speed to First Touch** | Time of First Contact - Time of Lead Creation | < 60 seconds |
| **Contact Rate** | (Leads Contacted / Total Leads) × 100 | > 95% |
| **Connect Rate** | (Conversations / Attempts) × 100 | > 30% |
| **Speed to Qualified** | Time from Lead to Qualification | < 24 hours |

### Dashboard Setup

Track these in real-time:
1. **Average response time** (trending over time)
2. **Response time by rep** (identify training needs)
3. **Response time by lead source** (optimize campaigns)
4. **Conversion rate by response time bucket** (prove ROI)

## The Contrarian View: Speed ≠ Generic

**Warning:** Fast but generic = spam. Fast AND personalized = conversions.

### Don't Do This:
```
Thank you for your interest. A representative will contact you shortly.
```

### Do This Instead:
```
Hey Sarah! Saw you're interested in our marketing automation for 
your real estate business. I help agents like you every day - 
quick question: are you more focused on lead gen or follow-up right now?
```

**Key Difference:**
- Uses their name
- References their business/industry
- Shows you know what they need
- Asks an engaging question
- Sounds like a human
