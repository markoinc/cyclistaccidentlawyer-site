## Overview

SMS lead generation is the process of collecting phone numbers from potential customers and using text messaging to engage, nurture, and convert them into buyers. With 98% open rates and 45% response rates, SMS outperforms every other marketing channel.

## Phase 2: The Opt-In Process

### Compliance Requirements (TCPA)

Before sending any marketing SMS, you MUST:

1. **Get Explicit Consent**
   - Written/electronic opt-in
   - Clear disclosure of what they'll receive
   - Frequency of messages disclosed

2. **Double Opt-In (Recommended)**
   ```
   Thanks for signing up! Reply YES to confirm 
   you want to receive texts from [Business]. 
   Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel.
   ```

3. **Include Required Disclosures**
   - "Msg & data rates may apply"
   - How to opt out (STOP)
   - Message frequency
   - Link to privacy policy

## Phase 6: Automation Best Practices

### Timing Optimization

**Best Times to Send:**
- 10am - 12pm (pre-lunch engagement)
- 2pm - 4pm (afternoon slump = phone check)
- 7pm - 9pm (evening downtime)

**Avoid:**
- Early morning (before 9am)
- Late night (after 9pm)
- Commute times (6:30-8:30am, 4-7pm)
- Mondays (lowest response rates)

### Frequency Guidelines

| Relationship Stage | Recommended Frequency |
|--------------------|----------------------|
| First 7 days | 3-4 messages |
| Weeks 2-4 | 2-3 messages/week |
| Ongoing | 4-6 messages/month |
| Re-engagement | 1 message, then wait |

**Warning Signs of Over-Messaging:**
- Opt-out rate exceeds 2%
- Response rates declining
- Complaints or negative replies

### Message Length

- **Ideal:** 160 characters or less (1 SMS segment)
- **Maximum:** 320 characters (2 segments)
- **Front-load value:** Most important info first
- **One CTA only:** Don't confuse with multiple asks

## Phase 8: Analytics & Optimization

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Delivery Rate | Delivered / Sent | >95% |
| Open Rate | Opened / Delivered | >90% |
| Click Rate | Clicks / Delivered | >10% |
| Response Rate | Replies / Delivered | >15% |
| Conversion Rate | Conversions / Delivered | >5% |
| Opt-Out Rate | Unsubscribes / Delivered | <2% |
| Revenue per SMS | Total Revenue / SMS Sent | Track trend |

### A/B Testing Ideas

1. **Message length** - Short vs. detailed
2. **Personalization** - Name vs. no name
3. **Emoji usage** - With vs. without
4. **CTA style** - Link vs. reply
5. **Send time** - Morning vs. evening
6. **Urgency** - Deadline vs. no deadline
7. **Tone** - Formal vs. casual

### Optimization Loop

1. **Measure** current performance
2. **Identify** lowest-performing messages
3. **Hypothesize** why they underperform
4. **Test** one variable at a time
5. **Implement** winning variations
6. **Repeat** continuously
