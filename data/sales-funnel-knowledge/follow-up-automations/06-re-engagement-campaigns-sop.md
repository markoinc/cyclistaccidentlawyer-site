## What is a Re-Engagement Campaign?

A re-engagement campaign is a targeted series of communications designed to "win back" subscribers or leads who have stopped engaging with your brand. These contacts once showed interest but have gone cold.

**Why It Matters:**
- Acquiring new customers costs 5-25x more than retaining existing ones
- Re-engaged customers spend 45% more than newly acquired ones
- A clean, engaged list improves deliverability for all emails

---

## When to Launch Re-Engagement

### Defining "Inactive"

| Business Type | Inactive Definition |
|---------------|---------------------|
| B2B SaaS | No login in 30+ days, no email open in 60+ days |
| B2B Services | No email engagement in 90+ days |
| E-commerce | No purchase in 90+ days, no email open in 45+ days |
| Media/Content | No email open in 60+ days |
| High-ticket B2B | No engagement in 120+ days |

### Segmentation Criteria

Create re-engagement segments based on:

**By Engagement Level:**
- Last email opened: > 30/60/90 days ago
- Last link clicked: > 30/60/90 days ago
- Last website visit: > 30/60/90 days ago
- Last purchase/conversion: > 90/180 days ago

**By Value Tier:**
- High-value (previous purchasers, high engagement history)
- Medium-value (some engagement, never converted)
- Low-value (minimal engagement, low intent signals)

## Advanced Re-Engagement Strategies

### Tiered Re-Engagement by Value

**High-Value Contacts (Previous Customers, High LTV):**
- More touchpoints (5-6 emails)
- Personalized offers
- Phone call from sales/success team
- Direct mail piece
- Longer timeline before removal

**Medium-Value Contacts:**
- Standard 4-email sequence
- Modest incentive if applicable
- 3-4 week timeline

**Low-Value Contacts:**
- Shorter sequence (2-3 emails)
- Generic messaging
- Quick removal if no response
- Focus resources elsewhere

### Multi-Channel Re-Engagement

```
Day 0: Email 1 - "We miss you"
Day 3: SMS - "Quick check-in" (if opted in)
Day 7: Email 2 - "What you've missed"
Day 10: Retargeting ads activated
Day 14: Email 3 - Special offer
Day 18: LinkedIn message (B2B)
Day 21: Email 4 - Goodbye
Day 28: Final SMS (if opted in)
Day 35: Remove from list if no engagement
```

## Post-Campaign Actions

### If They Re-Engage

1. **Immediately:**
   - Remove from inactive segment
   - Stop re-engagement emails
   - Add to "win-back" tracking

2. **Within 24 hours:**
   - Send welcome-back email
   - Highlight what they've missed
   - Offer next steps

3. **Ongoing:**
   - Monitor engagement closely
   - Adjust email frequency if needed
   - Note preferences in CRM

### If They Don't Re-Engage

1. **After final email + waiting period:**
   - Remove from marketing lists
   - Keep in CRM for reference
   - Note removal reason

2. **Optional - Long-term:**
   - Move to "cold" list
   - Annual check-in email (once per year)
   - Remove completely after 1-2 years
