Okay, here's a comprehensive SOP based on the YouTube video "How To Build A Sales Funnel In GoHighLevel (Full Walkthrough)" by Dom Bavaro.

**1. VIDEO OVERVIEW**

*   **Main Topic and Purpose:** This video provides a step-by-step guide on how to build a sales funnel within the GoHighLevel platform. The purpose is to enable viewers to create a functional and effective funnel for lead generation, nurturing, and conversion.

*   **Who This Tutorial Is For:** This tutorial is designed for GoHighLevel users, particularly those who are new to building sales funnels or who want a structured approach to creating them within the platform. It's beneficial for entrepreneurs, marketers, and agency owners who want to leverage GoHighLevel for their sales processes.

*   **Expected Outcomes:** By following this tutorial, viewers should be able to:
    *   Understand the basic structure of a sales funnel.
    *   Create a multi-step funnel within GoHighLevel.
    *   Design landing pages, thank you pages, and other funnel stages.
    *   Integrate forms for lead capture.
    *   Connect automation triggers and actions to the funnel.
    *   Track funnel performance and make data-driven improvements.

**2. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS**

**Step 1: Accessing the Funnels Section**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Log into your GoHighLevel account.
    2.  Navigate to the "Sites" section in the left-hand menu.
    3.  Click on "Funnels." This is where you'll create and manage your sales funnels.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** N/A
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Ensure you are in the correct sub-account if you manage multiple locations.
*   **Expected Result:** You should be on the Funnels dashboard, ready to create a new funnel.

**Step 2: Creating a New Funnel**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Click the "+ New Funnel" button in the top right corner of the Funnels dashboard.
    2.  You can either start from scratch ("Create Blank") or choose a pre-built template from the GoHighLevel template library. For this SOP, we will start from scratch.
    3.  Name your funnel appropriately (e.g., "Lead Magnet Funnel," "Webinar Registration Funnel").
    4.  Click "Create Funnel."
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** Funnel Name
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Choose a descriptive name that clearly indicates the funnel's purpose.
*   **Expected Result:** A blank funnel canvas is displayed where you can add steps.

**Step 3: Adding the First Step (Landing Page)**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Click the "+ Add New Step" button within the funnel canvas.
    2.  Name the step (e.g., "Landing Page," "Opt-in Page").
    3.  Set the "Path" for the URL of this page (e.g., "/free-ebook," "/webinar-registration"). This is the part of the URL that comes after your domain.
    4.  Click "Create Funnel Step."
    5.  Click the "Edit Page" icon (pencil icon) to open the page builder.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** Step Name, Path
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Keep the path short, relevant, and easy to remember.
*   **Expected Result:** The GoHighLevel page builder opens, allowing you to design the landing page.

**Step 4: Designing the Landing Page**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  You can either start with a blank page or choose a template from the GoHighLevel library.
    2.  Drag and drop elements (sections, rows, columns, elements) onto the page to build your design.
    3.  Key elements to include:
        *   **Headline:** A compelling headline that grabs attention.
        *   **Subheadline:** Explains the offer in more detail.
        *   **Image/Video:** A visual representation of the offer.
        *   **Form:** A form to capture the lead's information (name, email, phone number).
        *   **Call to Action (CTA) Button:** A button that encourages the visitor to take the desired action (e.g., "Download Now," "Register Today").
    4.  Customize the text, colors, fonts, and images to match your branding.
    5.  Connect the form to your GoHighLevel CRM.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:**
    *   **Form Integration:** Ensure the form is properly integrated with your GoHighLevel CRM to capture leads.
    *   **Button Action:** Set the button action to submit the form.
    *   **SEO Meta Data:** Configure the SEO title and description for the page.
*   **Tips/Warnings:**
    *   Focus on a clear and concise message.
    *   Make the form easy to fill out.
    *   Use high-quality images and videos.
    *   Ensure the page is mobile-responsive.
*   **Expected Result:** A visually appealing and functional landing page that captures leads.

**Step 5: Adding the Second Step (Thank You Page)**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Return to the Funnel dashboard.
    2.  Click "+ Add New Step."
    3.  Name the step (e.g., "Thank You Page," "Confirmation Page").
    4.  Set the "Path" (e.g., "/thank-you," "/registration-confirmed").
    5.  Click "Create Funnel Step."
    6.  Click the "Edit Page" icon to open the page builder.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** Step Name, Path
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Use a different path than the landing page.
*   **Expected Result:** The GoHighLevel page builder opens for the thank you page.

**Step 6: Designing the Thank You Page**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Choose a template or start from scratch.
    2.  Include a thank you message to acknowledge the lead's action.
    3.  Provide instructions on how to access the offer (e.g., "Check your email for the download link," "You will receive a confirmation email shortly").
    4.  Consider adding a secondary call to action (e.g., "Join our Facebook group," "Follow us on social media").
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** N/A
*   **Tips/Warnings:**
    *   Keep the thank you message concise and friendly.
    *   Clearly state the next steps for the lead.
*   **Expected Result:** A thank you page that confirms the lead's action and provides further instructions.

**Step 7: Setting the Form Redirect (Optional, but Recommended)**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Go back to the landing page within the funnel.
    2.  Click on the form element.
    3.  In the form settings, find the "Redirect to" option.
    4.  Choose "Funnel Step" and select the "Thank You Page" step you created.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** Form Redirect
*   **Tips/Warnings:** This ensures that users are automatically redirected to the thank you page after submitting the form.
*   **Expected Result:** After submitting the form on the landing page, users are automatically redirected to the thank you page.

**Step 8: Adding Automation (Optional, but Highly Recommended)**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Navigate to the "Automation" section in the left-hand menu.
    2.  Create a new workflow.
    3.  Set the trigger to "Form Submission" and select the form you used on the landing page.
    4.  Add actions to the workflow, such as:
        *   **Add Contact Tag:** Tag the contact to identify them as a lead from this specific funnel.
        *   **Send Email:** Send a welcome email with the promised offer (e.g., the ebook download link).
        *   **Add to Opportunity:** Create an opportunity in the sales pipeline.
        *   **Send SMS:** Send a text message to the lead.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:**
    *   **Trigger:** Form Submission
    *   **Actions:** Add Contact Tag, Send Email, Add to Opportunity, Send SMS
*   **Tips/Warnings:**
    *   Personalize the email and SMS messages.
    *   Use tags to segment your leads.
    *   Consider adding a delay before sending the email to avoid appearing spammy.
*   **Expected Result:** Automated actions are triggered when a lead submits the form, streamlining your sales process.

**Step 9: Testing the Funnel**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Visit the landing page URL.
    2.  Fill out the form with your own information.
    3.  Verify that you are redirected to the thank you page.
    4.  Check your email inbox to ensure you receive the welcome email.
    5.  Check your GoHighLevel CRM to confirm that the lead was added and tagged correctly.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** N/A
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Thoroughly test the funnel to identify and fix any issues.
*   **Expected Result:** The funnel functions as expected, capturing leads and triggering the automated actions.

**Step 10: Tracking and Optimization**

*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Monitor the funnel's performance using the GoHighLevel analytics dashboard.
    2.  Track metrics such as:
        *   **Page Views:** The number of times the landing page has been viewed.
        *   **Form Submissions:** The number of leads captured.
        *   **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of visitors who submit the form.
    3.  Use A/B testing to experiment with different headlines, images, and form fields to improve the conversion rate.
*   **Key Settings/Configurations:** N/A
*   **Tips/Warnings:** Continuously monitor and optimize the funnel to maximize its effectiveness.
*   **Expected Result:** Improved funnel performance and increased lead generation.

**3. KEY CONCEPTS EXPLAINED**

*   **Sales Funnel:** A visual representation of the customer journey, from initial awareness to final purchase. It typically consists of stages such as awareness, interest, decision, and action.
*   **Landing Page:** A standalone web page designed to capture leads or drive conversions.
*   **Thank You Page:** A page that confirms the lead's action and provides further instructions.
*   **Lead Magnet:** A valuable resource (e.g., ebook, checklist, template) offered in exchange for a lead's contact information.
*   **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of visitors who take the desired action (e.g., submitting a form, making a purchase).
*   **Automation:** The process of using software to automate repetitive tasks, such as sending emails and adding leads to the CRM.
*   **CRM (Customer Relationship Management):** A system for managing customer interactions and data throughout the customer lifecycle.

**4. TOOLS & RESOURCES MENTIONED**

*   **GoHighLevel:** The primary platform used for building the sales funnel.
*   **GoHighLevel Template Library:** A collection of pre-built funnel and page templates.
*   **GoHighLevel Page Builder:** A drag-and-drop editor for designing landing pages and thank you pages.

**5. COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID**

*   **Not having a clear offer:** Ensure your lead magnet is valuable and relevant to your target audience.
*   **Complicated forms:** Keep the form short and easy to fill out.
*   **Poor landing page design:** Use a clean and visually appealing design that focuses on the offer.
*   **Not testing the funnel:** Thoroughly test the funnel to identify and fix any issues.
*   **Ignoring analytics:** Monitor the funnel's performance and make data-driven improvements.
*   **Not following up with leads:** Implement automation to nurture leads and guide them through the sales process.
*   **Not optimizing for mobile:** Ensure your landing pages are mobile-responsive.
