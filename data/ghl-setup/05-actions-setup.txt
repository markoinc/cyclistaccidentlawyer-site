=====================================
BOT GOALS TAB → Actions Setup
=====================================

1. APPOINTMENT BOOKING ✅ (Primary Action)
   - Enable: YES
   - Calendar: Select your sales call calendar
   - Trigger phrase: "book a call", "schedule a call", "find a time", "set up a meeting"
   - Send booking link: YES (sends direct link instead of showing slots)
   - Pause bot after booking: YES

2. CONTACT INFO COLLECTION ✅
   - Enable: YES
   - Collect: Email, Phone, Name, State
   - Trigger: When they want info sent instead of booking
   - Example: "What's the best email to send that to?"

3. HUMAN HANDOVER ✅
   - Enable: YES
   - Trigger phrases: "talk to a human", "real person", "speak to someone"
   - Also trigger when: Bot is stuck, prospect is frustrated, complex questions
   - Notify: Your team's notification channel

4. STOP BOT ✅
   - Enable: YES
   - Trigger: When human takes over manually
   - Also stops: After appointment booked, after handover

5. TRIGGER WORKFLOW ✅
   - Enable: YES
   - Workflows to connect:
     a) "New Lead" workflow → triggers when contact info collected
     b) "Call Booked" workflow → triggers after appointment
     c) "Send Info Packet" workflow → when they want info without booking

6. AUTO FOLLOW UP ⚠️
   - Enable: OPTIONAL
   - Use if: Conversation goes cold (no response 24h+)
   - Message: "Hey, just checking in — still interested in learning about our MVA leads? Happy to answer any questions or find a time to chat."

7. TRANSFER BOT ❌
   - Enable: NO (unless you have multiple specialized bots)

=====================================
RECOMMENDED WORKFLOW TRIGGERS
=====================================

When prospect books call:
→ Send confirmation email
→ Add to "Sales Pipeline" in CRM
→ Send reminder 1 hour before

When prospect wants info (no booking):
→ Send info packet email
→ Add to "Nurture" sequence
→ Follow up in 3 days

When human handover:
→ Notify sales team (Slack/email)
→ Tag contact as "Needs Human"
