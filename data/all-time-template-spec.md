## CROSS-VERIFICATION PROCESS

When Marko provides all-time Chase transactions:

### Step 1: Import Raw Data
- Each account's CSV goes into a hidden "Raw" tab or separate verification sheet
- Label: `ðŸ” Verify: Business 4991`, `ðŸ” Verify: Personal 0068`, etc.

### Step 2: Match Against Monthly Sheets
For each transaction in the all-time CSV:
1. Find which month it belongs to
2. Verify it exists in the corresponding monthly sheet
3. Verify the amount matches
4. Flag any missing or mismatched transactions
