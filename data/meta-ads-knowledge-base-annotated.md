## Detailed Annotations by Section

### Campaign Setup

**"Use Sales objective for lead generation"**
- üß™ **EXPERIMENTAL** - This is a newer optimization trick
- **Why it works:** Meta's algorithm optimizes differently for Sales vs Leads
- **Risk:** May get fewer form fills but higher quality
- **Recommendation:** Test against Leads objective, compare quality

**"Enable CBO by default"**
- ‚úÖ **PROVEN** - Industry standard since 2019
- Meta actively pushes this; most experts recommend it

**"Use Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns (ASC)"**
- üß™ **EXPERIMENTAL** - Released 2022, still maturing
- Works great for e-commerce with lots of products
- **Risk:** Less control, needs lots of creative assets
- **Recommendation:** Test with 70/30 budget split vs manual campaigns

**"Start with broad targeting (no interest targeting)"**
- üß™ **EXPERIMENTAL** - Only works with mature pixel data
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Risky for new accounts** - Need conversion history first
- **Recommendation:** Start with interests/lookalikes, go broad once you have 500+ conversions

**"3-2-2 Structure"**
- ‚úÖ **PROVEN** - Charley T's framework, widely validated
- Safe starting point for testing

## Summary: What to Use When

### New Account (0-500 conversions)
Use these **PROVEN** strategies:
- Leads objective (not Sales)
- Interest targeting or 1-3% lookalikes
- CBO with 3-2-2 structure
- Start $50-100/day
- 7-day click, 1-day view
- UGC-heavy creative

### Established Account (500+ conversions)
Can experiment with:
- Sales objective for lead gen
- Broader targeting / Advantage+ Audience
- Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns
- Horizontal scaling

### High-Spend Account ($10K+/day)
Can go aggressive with:
- Full broad targeting
- 20-50 creatives/week
- Multiple campaign types running simultaneously
