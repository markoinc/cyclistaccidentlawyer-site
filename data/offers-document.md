## OFFER 2: LIVE TRANSFERS
**Best for:** Firms who want qualified prospects on the phone without doing the follow-up grind

### What You Get:
- All raw leads from Offer 1, PLUS
- our intake director's team (our intake team) calls and qualifies leads
- 5-minute call response on new leads
- 4 calls on day 1, 3 calls/day for 7 days
- Text sequence to increase contact rate
- Qualified, warm prospects transferred to YOUR phone
- About 30-40% of leads convert to transfers
- About 40-60% of transfers convert to signed cases

### Qualification Criteria (Customizable):
- Clear liability (not at-fault)
- Treatment within 21 days of accident
- Not currently represented (or confirmed switching)
- Insurance coverage verified
- Injury is monetizable (not just property damage)
- Within statute of limitations

### Pricing:
| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Raw Leads | $200-400/lead (by state) |
| Transfer Fee | $100 per qualified transfer |
| **Effective Cost Per Case** | **$2,000-3,000** |
