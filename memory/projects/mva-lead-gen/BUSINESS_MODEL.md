# MVA Lead Gen - Business Model

## Service Categories

The site covers **two complementary services** PI attorneys need:

### Category 1: Lead Vendors
Companies that generate and sell MVA/PI leads to law firms.
- Pay-per-lead models
- Retainer arrangements
- Exclusive vs shared leads

### Category 2: Outsourced Intake Services
Companies that handle intake calls and convert leads to signed cases.
- 24/7 call answering
- Lead qualification
- Retainer signing
- CRM integration

**Why Both?** Attorneys who buy leads need intake to convert them. These services are the full pipeline.
