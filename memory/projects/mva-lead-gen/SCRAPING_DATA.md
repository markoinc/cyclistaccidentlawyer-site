## PART 4: COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY

### FCC/TCPA 2025 Rules

**One-to-One Consent Rule:**
- Was supposed to take effect January 27, 2025
- **STATUS: Struck down / withdrawn** (per Eleventh Circuit ruling)
- Would have required consent "one seller at a time" instead of blanket consent
- Lead generators predicted lower availability, higher prices

**Key Compliance Concerns:**
- Non-compliant lead gen can result in bar complaints or FTC issues
- "Fake leads" fabricated by vendors = not valid consent
- Can't rely on lead generator to retain proof of consent
- Consent revocation rules still took effect April 2025

### Lead Quality Red Flags (from ZeroRisk warning)
- Foreign call centers using stolen healthcare info
- Fake profiles to generate leads
- Targeting specific medical conditions without valid consent
- Firms must thoroughly vet call center partners
