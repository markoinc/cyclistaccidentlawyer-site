# Proactive AI Workflows

## Overview
The key differentiator with Clawdbot isn't just having an AI assistant - it's making it **proactive**. This means setting up systems where AI takes action without being prompted.

## Core Principle
> "The magic is in making Clawdbot proactive, not just reactive."

## Key Workflows

### 1. Overnight Vibe Coding
Let Clawdbot work while you sleep:
- Schedule work sessions at 11 PM
- Create PRs for review (never push to production directly)
- Wake up to new features and improvements

### 2. Morning Briefs
Automated intelligence gathering:
- Local weather
- Industry news
- Calendar overview
- Tasks for the day
- Revenue-generating recommendations

### 3. Daily Research Reports
Afternoon deep dives:
- Concepts that improve the business
- Workflow optimizations
- Market trends
- Competitor analysis

### 4. Email Monitoring
Passive lead capture:
- Daily inbox summaries
- Highlight potential opportunities
- Draft responses
- Flag urgent items

## Implementation Checklist
- [ ] Set up morning brief at 8 AM
- [ ] Configure overnight work sessions
- [ ] Enable email monitoring
- [ ] Create daily research routine
