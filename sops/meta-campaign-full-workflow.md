## 1. Pre-Launch Planning

**Estimated Time:** 15-30 minutes

### Campaign Brief Checklist
Before starting, confirm:
- [ ] **Offer:** What are we promoting? (e.g., free consultation, lead magnet)
- [ ] **Target CPA:** What's our target cost per result? (If unknown, budget $30-100/lead for B2B)
- [ ] **Budget:** Daily/total campaign budget (Minimum recommended: $20-50/day to exit learning phase)
- [ ] **Audience:** Who are we targeting? (Be specific: job titles, interests, behaviors)
- [ ] **Landing Page:** URL ready and tested (visit it yourself, fill out the form)
- [ ] **Tracking:** Pixel installed, events firing (verified in Events Manager)

### Audience Research

**Step 1: Check Facebook Ad Library (Free Competitor Research)**
1. Go to: https://www.facebook.com/ads/library
2. In the search bar, type a competitor's name or keyword (e.g., "personal injury lawyer")
3. Filter by:
   - Country: United States
   - Platform: Facebook/Instagram
   - Media Type: Images and videos
4. Click on competitor ads to see:
   - Their ad copy (what hooks they use)
   - Their creative styles
   - How long ads have been running (longer = probably working)

**What to note:**
- ðŸ“ Common headlines they use
- ðŸ“ Pain points they address
- ðŸ“ Offers (free consultation, case review, etc.)
- ðŸ“ Image styles that appear repeatedly

**Step 2: Review Past Campaign Performance (If Available)**
1. Go to Ads Manager (business.facebook.com/adsmanager)
2. Set date range to last 90 days
3. Sort by "Cost per result" (lowest first)
4. Note which ads performed best:
   - What copy angles worked?
   - What creative types won?
   - What audiences converted?

### Copy Structure Rules

**Primary Text:**
- First 125 characters MUST hook (this is all they see before "See More")
- Total max: 500 characters
- Use line breaks for readability
- Emojis: Use sparingly (1-3 max), must be relevant

**Headline (40 characters max):**
âœ… Good: "Signed MVA Cases from $3k"

âŒ Bad: "The Best Legal Marketing Solution for Your Law Firm" (too long)

**Description (30 characters, optional):**
- Only shows on some placements
- Reinforces headline
- Examples: "Risk-free trial available" | "200+ firms trust us"

### âŒ Copy Mistakes to Avoid

| Mistake | Why It's Bad | Fix |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| No hook in first line | They'll scroll past | Put most compelling point first |
| Wall of text | Hard to read on mobile | Use line breaks every 1-2 sentences |
| Vague claims | "Best service" means nothing | Use specific numbers: "$3k per case" |
| Multiple CTAs | Confuses reader | One clear action per ad |
| ALL CAPS EVERYWHERE | Feels spammy, may get rejected | Use sparingly for ONE word max |
| Guaranteed results | Policy violation | Use "average" or "typical" |
| Mentioning Facebook | Policy violation | Never say "Facebook" in ad copy |

## 3. Creative Production

**Estimated Time:** 1-2 hours (depending on tools used)

### Creative Types (Priority Order)
1. **Static Images** - Fast to produce, easy to test (START HERE)
2. **Carousels** - Good for features/benefits list
3. **Video (UGC style)** - Highest engagement when done right
4. **Video (motion graphics)** - Good for explainers

### Static Image Specs

| Placement | Size (pixels) | Aspect Ratio | File Type |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|-----------|
| Feed (Primary) | 1080 x 1080 | 1:1 (square) | JPG or PNG |
| Stories/Reels | 1080 x 1920 | 9:16 (vertical) | JPG or PNG |
| Right Column | 1200 x 628 | 1.91:1 (landscape) | JPG or PNG |

**ðŸ’¡ Pro Tip:** Create 1:1 (1080x1080) as your primary. Meta will auto-crop for other placements. Only create 9:16 separately if you're investing heavily in Stories/Reels.

**File Requirements:**
- Max file size: 30MB
- Recommended: Under 1MB for fast loading
- Format: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with text

### Image Creative Guidelines

**Must-Haves:**
- âœ… Eye-catching hero image (faces perform well)
- âœ… High contrast (stands out in feed)
- âœ… Readable on mobile (text large enough)
- âœ… Brand colors consistent
- âœ… Clear value prop visible

**Avoid:**
- âŒ More than 20% text (may reduce delivery)
- âŒ Small text (unreadable on mobile)
- âŒ Cluttered design (too many elements)
- âŒ Stock photos that look fake
- âŒ Before/after images (policy violation in many categories)

**Video Technical Requirements:**
- Format: MP4 or MOV

The pixel is a piece of code on your website that:
- Tracks who visits your site from Meta ads

- Enables retargeting
- Helps Meta optimize ad delivery

## 7. Optimization Workflow

**Estimated Time:** 15-30 minutes daily, 1-2 hours weekly

### Weekly Optimization (1-2 hours)

**Step 1: Review Performance (30 min)**
1. Set date range to last 7 days
2. Sort by "Cost per result" (lowest first)
3. Identify:
   - ðŸ† Winners: Below target CPA
   - âš ï¸ Borderline: At target CPA
   - âŒ Losers: Above target CPA

**Step 2: Take Action**

| Performance | Action |
|-------------|--------|
| CPA < 50% of target | Increase budget 20-30% |
| CPA at target | Keep running, monitor |

### Understanding Learning Phase

**What is it?**
Meta needs ~50 conversions per week per ad set to optimize effectively.

**Learning Status:**
| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Learning | Gathering data | Normal, wait |
| Learning Limited | Not enough conversions | Increase budget, broaden targeting, combine ad sets |
| Active | Optimized | Good! Monitor only |

**How to Exit Learning Faster:**
- Higher daily budget ($50+/day per ad set)
- Broader audience (less restrictions)
- Optimize for more frequent event (if possible)
- Combine small ad sets into one

## 8. Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Ad Rejections

**Where to Check:**
Ads Manager â†’ Click rejected ad â†’ "Delivery" column â†’ Hover for reason

**Common Rejection Reasons:**

| Reason | What It Means | How to Fix |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| **Personal attributes** | Ad implies personal characteristics | Remove "you" statements like "Are you overweight?" |
| **Misleading claims** | Unsubstantiated promises | Add "results may vary", remove guarantees |
| **Landing page issues** | LP doesn't match ad or is broken | Ensure LP is live, matches ad content |
| **Prohibited content** | Violates advertising policies | Review Meta's ad policies, remove violating content |
| **Special category violation** | Didn't declare housing/credit/employment | Edit campaign, select correct special category |
| **Text in image** | >20% text in image | Reduce text overlay (Meta relaxed this but still matters) |

**Appeal Process:**
1. Click "Request Review" on rejected ad
2. Write brief explanation
3. Wait 24-48 hours
4. If denied again, create new ad following policies

---

### Low/No Delivery

**Check in this order:**

| Check | How | Fix |
|-------|-----|-----|
| **Ad approved?** | Delivery column shows "Active" | Wait for review or fix rejection |
| **Budget too low?** | Is daily budget < $10? | Increase to $20+ minimum |
| **Audience too small?** | Check "Potential Reach" in ad set | Broaden targeting |
| **Bid too low?** | Using bid cap? | Switch to "Highest volume" |
| **Schedule issue?** | Check start/end dates | Verify campaign is scheduled to run now |
| **Account issue?** | Check for notifications | Review account status, payment method |

---

### High CPA (Cost Per Lead)

**Diagnostic Questions:**
1. Is your landing page converting? (Check LP conversion rate independently)
2. Is your offer compelling?
3. Is the audience right?
4. Is creative resonating? (Check CTR)

**Fixes:**

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Fix |
|---------|--------------|-----|
| High CTR, high CPA | LP not converting | Improve landing page |
| Low CTR, high CPA | Ad not compelling | New creative/copy |
| High CPM, high CPA | Expensive audience | Broaden targeting |
| Normal metrics, high CPA | Market reality | Test different offer |

---

### Learning Limited

**Causes:**
- Budget too low for conversion volume
- Audience too narrow
- Too many ad sets splitting budget
- Optimizing for rare event

**Fixes:**
1. Increase budget (need ~50 conversions/week)
2. Combine multiple ad sets into one
3. Broaden targeting
4. Optimize for earlier funnel event (e.g., landing page view instead of lead)

---

### Account Disabled

**Immediate Steps:**
1. Check email for notification explaining why
2. Go to Account Quality: business.facebook.com/accountquality
3. Review specific violations listed
4. Click "Request Review" if you believe it's an error

**Prevention:**
- Never make medical/legal claims without disclaimers
- Always declare special ad categories
- Don't use engagement bait
- Don't change landing pages drastically after approval
- Keep payment method valid

## 9. Creative Refresh Cadence

| Creative Type | Refresh Frequency | Signs It's Time |
|---------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Static Images | Every 2-4 weeks | CTR dropping, frequency > 3 |
| Video | Every 3-6 weeks | Engagement dropping |
| Copy | Every 2-3 weeks | CTR declining |

### Signs Creative Needs Refresh
- CTR dropping week-over-week
- Frequency > 3 (prospecting) or > 6 (retargeting)
- CPM increasing without market changes
- Comments: "I keep seeing this ad"
- Engagement (likes, comments) declining

### Creative Testing Framework
Always have in rotation:
- 2-3 "control" ads (proven performers)
- 2-3 "test" ads (new variations)

Kill tests that underperform controls after sufficient data (~1000 impressions, 3+ days).
